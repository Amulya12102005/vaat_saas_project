Understanding EU OSS VAT

The One Stop Shop scheme simplifies VAT obligations for freelancers selling digital services to consumers across the EU.

What is OSS?

The OSS allows businesses to register for VAT in just one EU member state, even if they sell to customers in all 27 countries. It is mandatory for selling B2C digital services (e-books, software, hosting) exceeding €10,000 in pan-EU annual sales.

  • Single electronic registration.
  • Apply the VAT rate of the member state where the *consumer* is located.
  • One quarterly declaration and payment.
