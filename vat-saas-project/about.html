Our Mission: VAT Simplicity

As former freelancers themselves, the founders of VATMasterOSS experienced firsthand the confusion of the EU's One Stop Shop (OSS) rules[cite: 27]. Calculating different VAT rates for digital products across 27 countries was time-consuming and prone to error.

We built this tool to provide freelancers with the instant clarity they need to remain compliant without needing a degree in European tax law[cite: 27].

Our goal is to keep this tool static, fast, and always up-to-date with the official EU VAT rates[cite: 33, 56].
